ABOUT

VORRATH WOODWORKS

Vorrath Woodworks is a team of formally trained fine woodworkers from The Krenov School of Fine Furniture, in Fort Bragg California. Our training and experience gives us a leg up in the intricacies of fine woodworking, and the ability to push the boundaries.
